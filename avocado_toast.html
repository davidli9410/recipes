Avocado Toast

This recipe is a simple but delicious recipe for avocado toast, a favorite breakfast for many.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs(optional)
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Toast bread.
  2. Fry Egg until over easy.
  3. Mash up, or cut up avocado and lay on top of toast.
  4. Lay egg on top.
  5. Enjoy!
