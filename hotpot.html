Hot Pot

This is a recipe for hotpot, a traditional chinese dish where raw meats and vegetables are put into a boiling broth and eaten with various sauces. It's a very delicious and comforting meal!

Ingredients(None required)

  • Black fungus
  • Meat, such as lamb, beef, or pork
  • Tofu
  • Bean Curd
  • Sauce of some sort, for example sesame sauce or chinese vinegar
  • Rice Cakes
  • Instant Noodles, or Hand Pulled noodles
  • Veggies such as Bok Choy, Potatoes, or

Steps

  1. Prepare sauce.
  2. Prepare hot pot base, and boil
  3. Enjoy!
