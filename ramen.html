ramen

This recipe uses home made broth and noodles to achieve a rich, creamy and delicious ramen. Serves up to 4

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Pork Belly
  • Pork Bones
  • Scallion
  • Black Fungus
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt
  • Onions, Carrots, Celery (for broth)

Steps

  1. (at least 1 day in advance) Prepare broth by simmering water, veggies and pork bones together at low heat for a long period of time.
  2. Make ramen noodles using eggs and flour
  3. Boil pork belly, then sear after cooked
  4. Prepare garnishes: Black fungus and scallions, as well as soft-boiled egg.
  5. Assemble and enjoy!
